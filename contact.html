Contact Us

We want to provide others with an easy way to keep track of passwords. Like many people, we have accounts for school, loyalty programs, email, banking, and much more. It is not secure to use only one password, but remembering many different passwords is hard. As students interested in encryption and security, we have learned what makes passwords secure as well as how to hash, salt, and encrypt passwords and personal information using open source applications.
